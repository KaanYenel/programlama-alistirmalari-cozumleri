(Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit) Write a program that reads a Celsius degree in a
double value from the console, then converts it to Fahrenheit, and displays the
result. The formula for the conversion is as follows:

fahrenheit = (9 / 5) × celsius + 32

Hint: In Java, 9 / 5 is 1, but 9.0 / 5 is 1.8.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
(Celsius'u Fahrenheit'a çevirme) Konsoldan celcius derecesini double değeri olarak okuyan,
bunu fahrenheit'a çeviren ve sonucu ekrana yazdıran bir program yazın.

fahrenheit = (9 / 5) × celsius + 32

İpucu: Java'da 9 / 5, 1'dir ama 9.0 / 5, 1.8'dir.
